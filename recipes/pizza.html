Homemade Pizza Recipe

Pizza, a globally beloved dish, traces its origins to Naples, Italy. Its inception dates back to the 18th century when locals sought an affordable, quick meal. Flatbreads with various toppings, such as tomatoes, cheese, oil, and garlic, became a staple among Naples' working-class population. However, it was the addition of tomatoes, a New World ingredient, that revolutionized pizza. Once considered a novelty, the Margherita pizza, topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, gained popularity after Queen Margherita's endorsement in the late 19th century. This humble street food evolved into an international sensation, adapting to diverse tastes worldwide while remaining deeply rooted in its Neapolitan heritage.

As for the recipe, crafting a homemade pizza involves a few fundamental steps. Begin by preparing a dough from flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt. Let the dough rise before rolling it out into the desired crust thickness. Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, top it with cheese and your preferred toppings—be it classic pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, or innovative choices. Then, bake the pizza in a hot oven until the crust turns golden and the cheese melts into bubbly perfection. The result? A delicious homemade pizza, ready to be sliced and savored, each bite a testament to the simple yet delightful art of pizza-making.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of pizza toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired pizza crust thickness.
  7. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over the dough and add your favorite toppings.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the toppings.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
